About This Handbook

How to Use This Handbook

Volume 0 is the practical operating manual for the Jamieson Group laboratory — equipment, procedures, and lab conventions, written to let you complete a task correctly with minimal supervision.

🟢 QUICK SUMMARY Find your topic in the sidebar or the categories below. Each chapter is self-contained: safety first, then the steps, then what to do when something goes wrong.

This is not a textbook. Chapters are kept short and practical — background theory is included only where it changes what you actually do. If a chapter is missing something you needed, that's a gap worth reporting: see the maintenance guide in documentation/.
