Safety

● Draft v0.2 Updated 2026-07-17
QUICK SUMMARY
Standard organic chemistry lab safety is assumed knowledge — this chapter only covers what's specific to this lab. The one thing that matters most: TFA cleavage cocktails need a face shield, not just goggles.

PPE

  • Standard lab coat, gloves, and safety glasses for general work.
TFA CLEAVAGE COCKTAILS
Wear a face shield in addition to standard PPE when handling TFA cleavage cocktails.

Emergency procedures

Follow University of Glasgow / building health and safety procedures for spills, fire, and first aid — this handbook doesn't duplicate them.
