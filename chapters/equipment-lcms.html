Troubleshooting

A few habits worth keeping before you start swapping parts:

  • Check the basics first: mobile phase flowing with no air bubbles, LC pump pressure, ion source condition, MS vacuum.
  • Clearly define the problem (e.g. "loss of sensitivity", not just "it's not working") before changing things.
  • Change one variable at a time, and keep a record of what you tried.
