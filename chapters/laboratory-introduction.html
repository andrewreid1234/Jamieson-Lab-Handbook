Laboratory Introduction

🟢 QUICK SUMMARY The Jamieson Group is based in the Advanced Research Centre (ARC), University of Glasgow, working on peptide chemistry and chemical biology. This chapter covers who's who, where things live, and how the group communicates — the machines and procedures have their own chapters.

Location

Communication

The group mainly communicates by email and WhatsApp. Everyone sits together in the shared office/write-up area, so most day-to-day questions are answered in person.
