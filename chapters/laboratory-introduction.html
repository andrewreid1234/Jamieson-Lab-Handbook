Laboratory Introduction

● Draft v0.2 Updated 2026-07-18
QUICK SUMMARY
The Jamieson Group is based in the Advanced Research Centre (ARC), University of Glasgow, working on peptide chemistry and chemical biology. This chapter covers who's who, where things live, and how the group communicates — the machines and procedures have their own chapters.

Location

The Jamieson Group is based in the Advanced Research Centre (ARC), University of Glasgow.

Communication

The group mainly communicates by email and WhatsApp. Everyone sits together in the shared office/write-up area, so most day-to-day questions are answered in person.

Lab books & data

Most members keep a hard-copy lab book, which stays in the lab.
